The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investing in solar and wind energy solutions to secure a sustainable and environmentally friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"All human beings are entrepreneurs. When we were in the caves, we were all self-employed, finding our food, feeding ourselves. That’s where human history began. As civilization came, we suppressed it. We became labor because they stamped us, 'You are labor.' We forgot that we are entrepreneurs."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh

Bangladesh Launches New Satellite


Date: 11/01/2025 Time: 10:30 PM

The Bangabandhu Satellite-1 (Bangabandhu-1) is the first Bangladeshi geostationary communications and broadcasting satellite. It is named after the first president of Bangladesh, Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman. It was manufactured by Thales Alenia Space and launched on 12 May 2018 from Kennedy Space Center, USA.
